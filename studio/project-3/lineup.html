This Weeks Artists...

Dayglow is the brainchild of Sloan Struble, a musician from Austin, Texas. Born in 1996, Sloan has been making music and performing live since his teens.

Heavily influenced by indie and alternative rock, Dayglow has combined these sounds with pop and electronic elements to create vibrant and upbeat music.

Declan Benedict McKenna is an English singer, songwriter, and musician. He initially gained recognition for winning the Glastonbury Festival's Emerging Talent Competition in 2015.

Since then he has released the albums What Do You Think About the Car, Zeros, and What Happened to the Beach?.

Born in Cambridge as Charlotte Emma Aitchison, Charli XCX is a singer, songwriter, model and actress. Daughter of a Scottish father and Indian mother, she studied at UCL's Slade School of Fine Art in London.

A forerunner in the experimental electronic music scene, her new album brat is out now.

The Strokes are an American rock band from New York City, New York, United States, formed in 1998. The band rose to fame in the early 2000s as a leading group in garage rock/post-punk revival.

The band consists of Julian Casablancas (lead vocals), Nick Valensi (lead guitar), Albert Hammond, Jr.
